## 55+ Years of Precision Plastic Manufacturing Excellence

Pro Plastics Inc. has been a trusted leader in custom plastic manufacturing since 1968. Based in Linden, New Jersey, we've spent over five decades perfecting our craft and serving industries that demand the highest standards of quality and precision.

## Complete Manufacturing Capabilities Under One Roof

Unlike many competitors who specialize in only one area, we offer comprehensive plastic solutions:
- Precision CNC horizontal and vertical machining with tight tolerances for complex geometries
- Full-service custom fabrication including routing, milling, drilling, and punching
- Comprehensive material distribution with 1000+ engineering-grade plastics in stock
- Rapid prototyping from concept to production-ready parts

This means customers work with a single trusted partner from design through delivery—no coordination between multiple vendors, no finger-pointing when issues arise.

## Unmatched Material Expertise

With over 1,000 materials available, our team helps customers select the optimal plastic for their specific application. We work with high-performance materials like PEEK, Ultem, and Torlon; engineering plastics like Delrin and Nylon; fluoropolymers including PTFE and PVDF; and standard materials like UHMW, HDPE, and Polycarbonate. Our material experts understand chemical resistance, temperature ratings, FDA compliance, and mechanical properties—guiding customers to the best solution for their unique requirements.
